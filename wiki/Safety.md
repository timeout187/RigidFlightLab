# Safety and Academic-Use Statement

RigidFlightLab is an **academic simulation** built for **published-
benchmark reproduction** and **numerical methods education only**.

## What this project IS

- An offline, educational 6-DOF rigid-body flight dynamics simulator.
- A tool for reproducing the qualitative results (trajectory shape,
  spin decay, epicyclic pitch/yaw motion, angle-of-attack behavior) of
  a published academic paper on spinning-projectile dispersion
  analysis.
- A teaching aid for numerical integration methods (RK4, adaptive
  Runge-Kutta, etc.) applied to rigid-body dynamics.

## What this project IS NOT

- **NOT** a fire-control system.
- **NOT** a targeting tool.
- **NOT** an operational artillery calculator.
- **NOT validated for real-world fire-control use.**
- Does **not** provide real-world firing recommendations, aim
  solutions, weapon-deployment guidance, or target-engagement
  capability.
- Contains **no** target-coordinate input, aim correction, live-fire
  recommendation, artillery firing-table generation, military
  operational-planning features, or optimization for maximum range or
  accuracy.

## Intended audience

Graduate/PhD-level students and researchers studying 6-DOF rigid-body
flight dynamics, exterior ballistics theory, and numerical simulation
methods.

## Disclaimer

This software is provided for educational and research purposes only.
The authors make no representation regarding its accuracy or fitness
for any operational, military, or safety-critical purpose. Any use
outside of academic research and education is explicitly out of
scope and unsupported.
